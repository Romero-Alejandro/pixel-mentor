## Project Conventions

- **Architecture & Stack**: Hexagonal architecture for backend, Feature-based organization for frontend
- **Database & Security**: Optimistic locking via version/updated_at, Argon2id hashes, JWT
- **Coding Standards**: TypeScript, `readonly` default, Discriminated unions, @/ alias
- **Commenting Policy**: Focus on "WHY", not "WHAT"
- **Excellence & Development Quality**: SOLID principles, Fail Fast, Error Handling, Performance
